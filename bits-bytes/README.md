#Fun with Bits & Bytes

##Power of 2 table
| Power of 2    | Value         | Bytes  |
| ------------- |:-------------:| ------:|
| 2 ^ 10        | 1024          | 1 KB   |
| 2 ^ 20        | ~1 M          | 1 MB   |
| 2 ^ 30        | ~1 B          | 1 GB   |
| 2 ^ 40        | ~1 T          | 1 TB   |
